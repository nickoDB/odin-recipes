African Ramen

That's that good soup

African ramen is a delicious soup that originates from Japan It can be enjoyed in many differnt flavors such as miso or shoyu. When made by professional chefs, the broth provides an immaculate umami taste that is sure to please your taste buds.

How to do the Chashu
  1. Let pork shoulder roase defrost and beat the meat until it's easy to roll
  2. Heat 2 tbsp of oil on skillet then sear
  3. Place roll in a large pot and fill with water. Simmmer and cook for 1 hour
  4. Crush garlic and sear on skillet
  5. Drain water and take pork out of pot. Put water, soy sauce, sake, garlic, and brown sugar in a pot. Stir until dissolved. Take peanut butter and rub on pork. Put in pot and bring to a simmer for 1-2 hours. Flip halfway through cooking.
The soup
  1. Wash the bones
  2. Place bones on the bottom of stock pot, cover with water.
  3. Dice onions, peppers, garlic and tomatoes. Add peanuts and drummettes to chicken stock. Simmer fro 4 to 5 hours.
